Origin Story

Hi, my name is Nadia.

I was born in a small country in Central America, worked towards going to France to have better opportunities in life and did it at 17 years old by myself. Since then, I have travelled the world to live, study varied subjects and work.

Inspired by my incredible grandfather, who devoted his entire life to helping others, I decided to dedicate my work to effecting positive impact. Recently, I completed an MBA to increase my chances of achieving this goal.

Now, one of my endeavours is to support purpose-driven people and entrepreneurs, ignite the same positive spark among other people and leverage our common knowledge to help each other and our communities.
