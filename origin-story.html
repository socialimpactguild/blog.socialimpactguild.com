Origin Story

Hi, my name is Nadia.

I was borned in Central America, worked towards going to France to have better opportunities in life and did it at 17 years-old by myself. Since then, I have travelled the world to live, study varied subjects and work.

Inspired by the life of my incredible grand-father, who dedicated his entire life to helping others, I decided to focus my work towards effecting positive social outcomes. I want to create as many projects as possible to help people and their communities. Recently, I completed an MBA to increase my chances of achieving this goal.

Now, one of my endeavours is to create a support system for social entrepreneurs. Indeed, I had the chance to meet with some of them during my journey and they were all extremely passionate about generating lasting, large-scale social benefits. So, I felt an immediate strong connection with them.

Since they all shared challenges and drivers, I decided I would do something to help them, ignite the same spark amongst other people and leverage our common knowledge to support each other and our communities.

The Social Impact Guild aims to help social entrepreneurs gain visibility and leverage their cumulative experience to face their day-to-day challenges. It would also provide a safe space where social entrepreneurs can share their feelings and stories. If you are a social entrepreneur or someone who wants to enable social impact, you can express your interest by completing this short survey (2-3 mins): https://socialimpactguild.com/survey

Thank you for reading this post. I am looking forward to connecting with you soon!
