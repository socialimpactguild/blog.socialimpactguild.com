OACC is Putting People at the Centre of Architecture

Architectural projects can often occur without much collaboration with the people whom they will affect. Indeed, community members usually provide inputs during the projects' early stages and do not participate in the design of solutions nor the implementation process. Human-centred design is already commonly used in various industries, but not yet in this space. Learn more about how the Open Architecture Collaborative Canada addresses community challenges by designing with the people they hope to serve through this short interview with Program Director Marveh Farhoodi.
